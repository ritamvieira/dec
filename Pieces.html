  • The I Block

    • The I block is an incredibly useful block. This is the only block that can give you a Tetris. Holding onto these is key.
  • The L Block

    • People often confuse the L block and the J block. The L block is great at filling in sections with a tab that are found on the left of the screen.
  • The J BLock

    • People often confuse the L block and the J block. The J block is great at filling in sections with a tab that are found on the right of the screen.
  • The O Block

    • The O block is a good block to place on the base of the screen, as it is a good jumping off point. Other than that, use it to fill 2 wide gaps.
  • The Z Block

    • The Z block and the S block are often confused. Use a Z block to fill that awkward space that can sometimes pop up.
  • The S Block

    • The Z block and the S block are often confused. Use an S block to fill that awkward space that can sometimes pop up.
  • The T Block

    • This block can be used as either an S or a Z block in order to flip what block is being stacked. It can also fill T shaped areas.
