How I Got Started with TETRiS

I began playing Tetris early 2018. I began playing on Tetris Friends, which was shut down on May 31st, 2019. This game used a leveling system that allowed me to have a numerical value for my skill level. I reached level 17/20. After this, I quickly became addicted, and went on to play the best Tetris game of all time, Tetris 99. This game allowed me to show my skills to 98 other players at a time. After the shut down of Tetris Friends, I went on to play normal Tetris on the Tetris website. This game does not offer multiplayer, but does provide the classic Tetris experience.
